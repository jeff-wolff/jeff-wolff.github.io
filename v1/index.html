Hello. I'm Jeff Wolff and I'm a freelance web designer based in San Diego, California. I've been designing and building websites for around 3 years now. All of my websites are coded using tableless XHTML/CSS. I design on a 24" iMac coupled with Adobe Photoshop for designing websites and Textmate for building them.

I strive to create sites that are elegant, engaging, and simple to use. I design websites based on a user-centered perspective. Users will be able to find what they want quickly and easily on your website. A better user interface results in more sales, returning visitors, and even leads. Furthermore, I test your website on a plethora of browsers (Internet Explorer, Firefox, Chrome, Safari, Opera) on both Mac and PC to make sure it looks great in any browser you use. Your website will look great and also function great, guaranteed.
